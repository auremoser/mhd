Divination + Data Journalism


"You should not work for a non-profit, because the for-pro[phe]ts can predict the future"

And there is something to be said, as comical as that sounds, for the idea that journalist developers are expected to divine the future with data. In many ways I have a ritual and practice that I use in approach to my work, and so I'm going to go over some of that today

"Developer-Journalist"


Technologist working with data and news apps development.

Developer working on visual representations of abstract info to reinforce human cognition.

Data Journalist designing methods for communitcating quantitative information with qualitative nuance.

Goal of News Applications

Adopt a triangle methodology: simply communicate data fact and avoid narrative fiction

Pyramids To Predict Success



I think often, our process aligns with this pyramid for greater information. This kind o simple pictographic iconography is the kind of intuitive legibility we like to infuse into our applications. With an emphasis on preparation, design planning and data cleaning, you can end up with a visual that is more informative than forboding, something that builds the foundation for a good narrative, and fractalizes throughout a story that is greater than it's development parts.

Approach to Visualizations


  • Understand your audience
  • Understand your data and desired narrative
  • Define a visual style and register
  • Build + Test
